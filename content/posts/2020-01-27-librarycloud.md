[CURIOSity Digital Collections][] and [Harvard Digital Collections][] are both built upon APIs available from [LibraryCloud][] but this is only mentioned in passing in [some][] [docs][]. Visually beautiful things can be built on top of APIs.

[CURIOSity Digital Collections]: https://curiosity.lib.harvard.edu
[Harvard Digital Collections]: https://library.harvard.edu/digital-collections
[some]: https://curiosityservice.library.harvard.edu/launch-maintain
[docs]: https://library.harvard.edu/services-tools/harvard-digital-collections
[LibraryCloud]: https://wiki.harvard.edu/confluence/display/LibraryStaffDoc/LibraryCloud

Back on January 6th I went to a talk on LibraryCloud and met the team. They're doing great work and it's [open source][]!

[open source]: https://github.com/harvard-library/librarycloud
